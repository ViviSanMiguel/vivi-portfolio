Projects

M!CLOSET

Challenge: Solving a daily problem

M!Closet is a closet management tool that allows you to organize your wardrobe, so you can get the most out of it. As an example, it will allow you to know how many blue jeans you have, give you suggestions on what outfit you should be wearing and which clothes match well together so you are always in style!

MALASPINA

Challenge: Responsive Editorial Design

If you are looking for a modern magazine which is influenced by Latin American culture, I can tell you some good news: you just found it! MalaSpina is covering current news, ranging from music to politics that will catch all of your attention. i wanted to incorporate the magazines core value in the design by making it not only modern, responsive and intuitive, but also eye-catching and with a lot of love for details.

BURNING MAN 2019

Challenge: Curated Event Microsite

The old website of the worldwide renown Burning Man event was confusing and wasted too much time of the user to purchase their tickets. Therefore, my challenge was to change that with this new redesign that I have done. The new microsite is simple, practical and responsive. the hassle-free experience will make the anticipation for the event even more thrilling!

About UX/UI

UX design refers to user experience design, while UI design stands for user interface design. Both of these are crucial to an IT product and need to work closely together. A UI Designer makes technology easy and intuitive for people to use. A UX Designer connect business goals to user’s needs through a process of testing and refinement towards that which satisfies both sides of the relationship.
